BrickHeadz

Brickheadz combine three elements which make them my perfect collectible:

  • low poly 3D models
  • nerdy pop culture characters
  • Lego! (this may be the extent of my mid-life crisis)

My collection is primarily made up of Chinese knockoff LEGO MOCs purchased on AliExpress. This definitely keeps the cost down but also means about 3 months on average for delivery.

If you stumble upon this page and want to unload any models I don't have yet, please get in touch.
